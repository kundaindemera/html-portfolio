Top movies of all time

This list is based on my taste in movie, the movies are from different genres so they won't be in any particular order. It obviously won't be in order because ranking them is so difficult since each movie has different charecteristics that could contribute to it being at the top.
Hope you try out a few for yourself.
LET'S GET INTO IT!!!!

  1. The Wolf of Wall Street

    2. Most people only know this movie because of the nudity, drugs, crazinnes and the beautiful Margot Robbie. But it's a really beautiful true life story about investing and going from rags to riches

  2. Inception

    3. Now this movie is AMAZING. The actors, directors, special effects and story line are all top tier. It will make you see dreams and reality differently.

  3. The Social Network

    4. If you want to know how Mark started with Facebook then this is the movie for you. It shows you how this man thought as a young man and the things he went and put other people through to build this billion dollar company.

  4. Shrek

    5. THE HUMOUR IN THESE MOVIES WAS AHEAD OF ITS TIME. I would rewatch them as many times as possible. A different, but needed, approach to fairy tale stories. If you haven't watched them then what are you doing with your life.

  5. Transformers (the ones directed by Michael Bay)

    6. Great storyline, amazing special effects and some cool ass cars. Ahead of its time for real. My children's children are definitely going to watch this.

  6. Spider-Man

    7. Marvel really created something beautiful here. I still have moments where I wish I was bitten by a radioactive spider and turned into your favourite neighbourhood spider. The original trilogy will forever be the best, but the animated spiderverse edition is a close second. Spiderman is not just for kids!!

  7. Creed

    8. Your modern version of the Rocky movies. Extremely amazing. A great combination of actors. Makes you want to start boxing lol.

  8. Fatherhood

    9. Very emotional movie about a man who had to raise his daughter on his own because his wife didn't make it after giving birth. So emotional that it's one of the only movies that made me cry. It is a must watch for sure.

  9. Avengers

    10. Best superheroe team(after the Justice League of course). The detail in each movie is amazing, all of them connected leading up to an amazing end to an era. Thanos is by far the most logical villain ever, diabolical but with valid reasoning.But in order to really understand the series you would have to watch individual movies based on the members of the avengers.
